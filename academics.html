Academics

A CBSE curriculum built stage by stage

From foundational literacy in Primary to stream specialisation at Senior Secondary — a clear, connected academic path for every learner.

Assessment

Examination policy

Continuous & Comprehensive Evaluation

Students are assessed through periodic tests, projects, class participation and term examinations, in line with CBSE guidelines, so progress is tracked well before final boards.

Term Examinations

Two major term examinations are held each academic year, covering the full syllabus taught up to that point, with report cards issued after each term.

Board Examinations (Grade X & XII)

Grade X and XII students appear for CBSE board examinations, supported by structured revision plans, mock tests and one-on-one mentoring in the lead-up months.

Promotion Criteria

Promotion to the next grade is based on minimum attendance and passing marks across the year's assessments, as per CBSE norms.

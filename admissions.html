How it works

Admission procedure

Step 1

Enquiry & campus visit

Reach out to the admissions desk or visit the campus to understand the school, meet faculty and view facilities.

Step 2

Application form

Submit the completed application form along with the required documents.

Step 3

Entrance interaction

An age-appropriate entrance assessment or interaction, used to understand the child's readiness for the grade.

Step 4

Confirmation & enrolment

On selection, complete the enrolment formalities and fee registration to confirm the seat.

Age Eligibility

Age criteria for each grade follow CBSE and state government norms as on the academic year's cut-off date. Please confirm the exact cut-off date for your child's grade with the admissions office.

Documents Required

  • Birth certificate (original + copy)
  • Transfer certificate from previous school (if applicable)
  • Previous grade report card / mark sheet
  • Aadhaar card of the student
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Address proof of parent/guardian

Entrance Examination

Applicants beyond the entry-level grade undergo a short, age-appropriate assessment in core subjects along with an interaction, helping us place each child at the right level.

Withdrawal Procedure

Parents seeking withdrawal should submit a written request to the school office, clear any outstanding dues, and collect the Transfer Certificate as per school policy.

Fee structure

Contact Administration for Fee Structure

Fee details vary by grade and are shared directly by our admissions team. Reach out and we'll respond with complete information.

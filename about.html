About Linga Global School

A decade of building future-ready global citizens

From a founding vision of rural empowerment to a CBSE-affiliated campus with modern smart classrooms — here is our story, our people, and the values that hold it all together.

Our story

Established 2015, built for the next generation

Linga Global School was established in 2015 in Krishnankoil, Tamil Nadu, with academic support from Maharishi Vidya Mandir, Chennai. Affiliated to CBSE, New Delhi (Affiliation No. 1930701, School Code 55610), the school was founded on a simple conviction: that knowledge is power, and every child — regardless of geography — deserves access to a world-class, globally-minded education.

Today LGS is home to a full CBSE curriculum from the primary years through Senior Secondary, delivered across smart classrooms, science and computer labs, and a campus designed with safety and wellbeing at its core.

Founder's legacy

Kalvivallal Thiru T. Kalasalingam

"A single educated child can lift an entire village. Give them the tools, and step back to watch the future arrive."

Honoured with the title Kalvivallal — meaning patron of education — Thiru T. Kalasalingam has spent decades building institutions that bring rigorous, modern education within reach of rural and first-generation learners. His work threads together three enduring commitments: education as the great equaliser, technology as a bridge rather than a barrier, and rural communities as places of untapped potential rather than an afterthought.

Under his guidance, institutions in the Kalasalingam ecosystem have introduced computer-aided learning, skill-based training and industry-linked programmes years ahead of mainstream adoption — always with an eye on students who would otherwise never have had the opportunity. Linga Global School stands as a continuation of that mission: a genuinely global curriculum, delivered with the same rural-first conviction that has defined his life's work.

What drives us

Vision, mission & core values

Vision

To nurture future-ready global citizens by providing a holistic, value-based education that fosters intellectual curiosity, creativity, and leadership.

Mission

To deliver rigorous, CBSE-aligned academics; to build character through discipline and empathy; to make technology a genuine learning partner; and to keep every child safe, seen and supported.

Core Values

Integrity, curiosity, respect, resilience and community — the five threads woven through every lesson, sport and celebration on campus.

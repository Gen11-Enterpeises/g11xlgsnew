Who we are

Knowledge is Power — and we build it deliberately.

Education is the key to unlocking potential and driving progress. At Linga Global School, we believe knowledge empowers individuals, and with the right guidance, every student can achieve excellence. Our commitment is to cultivate wisdom, nurture talent, and prepare students for a bright future.

Our Mission

The Founder

Kalvivallal Thiru T. Kalasalingam

"Education is the truest form of empowerment — it must reach every village, every child, every future."

A visionary institution builder revered as Kalvivallal — patron of education — Thiru T. Kalasalingam dedicated his life to widening access to quality learning across rural Tamil Nadu. Long before "EdTech" became a word, he championed technology-driven classrooms, believing that a child's postcode should never limit their potential. His institutions have opened doors for thousands of first-generation learners, blending rigorous academics with a deep commitment to community upliftment. Linga Global School carries this legacy forward — combining his belief in rural empowerment with a genuinely global, future-ready curriculum.
